The Best Movies Ever Made

according to Emil


A small list of my favourite movies of all time

Interstellar

A fantastic movie by Christopher Nolan, that contains plenty of twists and turns, and really bends your mind in a new way.

The Interview

A absolutely hilarious movie, about two guys that get the opportunity to interview Kim Jong-Un. Very controversial, but also very funny.

Inception

Another nolan movie, another mind bender. This one literally takes you into the mind of other people, and is a very interesting movie.
